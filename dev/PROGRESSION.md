# Hollow Reflections — Unit Progression v0.9

## Level caps

| Tier | Maximum level |
|---|---:|
| 1★ | 20 |
| 2★ | 40 |
| 3★ | 60 |
| 4★ | 70 |
| 5★ | 90 |

A standard evolution requires the maximum level for its current tier. Forced story evolutions may explicitly waive that rule.

## Experience

Every participating unit receives the quest's listed XP award. XP is deterministic and carries across multiple level-ups. The amount needed for the next level is `60 + (level × 20)`. At the current rarity cap, XP is held at zero until an evolution raises that cap.

## Evolution requirements

Hale is the exception to the standard evolution rule. His Chapter Six transformation automatically and permanently promotes him from 3★ to 4★, regardless of level, and consumes no Challenge materials. The moment immediately unlocks his true Hollowglass kit.

Hale will eventually have a separate 5★ form. Its evolution method, name, artwork, and kit are intentionally left undesigned for now.

Cinnia’s planned awakening is provisionally listed as:

- Level 70
- 10 Ember Challenge Crests
- 1 Feastkeeper’s Seal

Cinnia’s listed materials are placeholders for the future Challenge map and can be renamed or rebalanced when that map is designed.

All other standard evolutions continue to require the current tier's maximum level and their listed Challenge materials.
