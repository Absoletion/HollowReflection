# Cinnia — 4★ Character Reference

**Status:** Canonical direction approved from `Cinnia 4 star Reference.png`  
**Element:** Fire  
**Role:** Healer  
**Core fantasy:** A warm, absent-minded battle-cook whose food and field medicine keep the guild standing.

## Character read

Cinnia should be immediately welcoming. Hale is cut from dark angles and controlled tension; Cinnia is built from warm curves, open expressions, practical cooking shapes, and sunlit color. Her design is not a joke costume. She is a legitimate adventurer whose craft happens to be cooking, nourishment, and improvised field care.

## Canonical appearance

- Young adult woman with a friendly, round-cheeked anime face.
- Bright green eyes.
- Warm freckles across the bridge of her nose and upper cheeks.
- Vivid red-orange hair arranged in high curled twin-tails.
- Small braids integrated into each twin-tail.
- Wheat ornaments and small apple-shaped hair clasps.
- Cream-yellow field dress with a broad, readable skirt silhouette.
- Brown leather corset-harness and belts that make the dress field-ready.
- Reinforced leather bracers and brown boots with practical metal shin plates.
- Orange-red flame, wheat, and apple embroidery concentrated near the hem and selected costume accents.
- Long-handled blackened-iron frying pan as her 4★ evolved weapon and casting focus.
- Engraved apple-flame emblem on the pan, glowing orange when magic is active.

Preserve the generated reference's overall silhouette, palette, hair, dress, and weapon direction. Add freckles consistently to every portrait and model sheet.

## Personality

Cinnia is cheerful, nurturing, stubborn, and a little ditzy.

Her absent-minded habits include:

- Searching for a utensil that is already in her hand.
- Entering a room and forgetting why, then leaving with three unrelated useful things.
- Losing the thread of a conversation when she notices someone has skipped a meal.
- Preparing an excellent solution to the wrong minor problem.
- Mixing up formal terminology while understanding the practical answer perfectly.

Her ditziness must never erase her competence. When someone is hungry, injured, poisoned, frightened, or in immediate danger, Cinnia becomes focused and remarkably exact. She remembers allergies, favorite foods, medication timings, old injuries, and the emotional tells of every regular guild member.

The comedy is affectionate and character-driven—not infantilizing. She is not unintelligent, helpless, careless with patients, or unaware of danger.

## Voice

- Warm, direct, and conversational.
- Often begins in the middle of a thought.
- Uses cooking metaphors without forcing them into every sentence.
- Treats feeding someone as both care and tactical preparation.
- Can become unexpectedly firm when a patient refuses help.

### Sample lines

- “I brought bandages. And soup. You asked for one of those. Probably both now.”
- “Nobody fights hollow on an empty stomach.”
- “Hold still. I know exactly what I’m doing—I’ve only forgotten what it’s called.”
- “The pan was in my hand the whole time, wasn’t it? Good. That saves us a minute.”
- “Eat first. Bleed later. In that order, always.”
- “I forgot why I came over here. Oh! You look pale. That’ll do.”

## Combat expression

- **Idle:** buoyant and attentive, with small distracted glances toward the pan or supply pouch.
- **Skill:** quick practical motion, as if serving something at exactly the right moment.
- **Art:** opens her stance and distributes warmth across the party.
- **Burst:** turns the battlefield into a brief full-course feast; cheerful spectacle, abundant firelight, and a strong protective center.
- **Hit reaction:** surprised first, determined immediately afterward.

Her Fire should feel like hearth flame rather than uncontrolled destruction: orange, amber, cream-white cores, rising sparks, round pan arcs, wheat shapes, and apple-flame emblems.

## Motif hierarchy

1. **Primary:** blackened cookware and hearth flame.
2. **Secondary:** wheat and nourishment.
3. **Personal emblem:** apple inside a flame.

Do not distribute all three motifs equally across every surface. The pan owns the apple-flame emblem; the dress hem carries wheat and flame rhythm; hair ornaments use small wheat/apple notes.

## Restrictions

- Do not restore the full old headscarf unless a separate costume calls for it.
- Do not revert the weapon to a generic staff, wand, or conventional mace.
- Do not remove her freckles.
- Do not make her dress look too delicate to travel or fight in.
- Do not make her visual comedy sloppy or unhygienic.
- Do not portray ditziness as medical incompetence.
- Do not give her Hale's gothic violet, cathedral-circle, or Hollowglass language.
