# Act 1 Mission Map

Chapter One now uses a ten-node mission framework. Versions 1-1 through 1-3 are playable; 1-4 through 1-10 reserve stable locations for the dialogue and guided-tutorial pass.

| Mission | Working title | Teaching purpose | Status |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1-1 | Guild Entrance Exam | Skill activation | Playable |
| 1-2 | Second Construct | Arts Energy and Arts | Playable |
| 1-3 | Field Readiness | Burst activation | Playable |
| 1-4 | Meet the Garrison | Town and mission navigation | Planned |
| 1-5 | The Quest Board | Side missions | Planned |
| 1-6 | First Contract | Party formation | Planned |
| 1-7 | Roadside Hollow | Enemy elements | Planned |
| 1-8 | Return to the Guild | Experience and levels | Planned |
| 1-9 | The Still Basin | Summoning | Planned |
| 1-10 | Charter Day | Chapter trial | Planned |

Mission progress is stored separately from the legacy chapter spine so existing saves retain their completed story state. Each playable mission has fixed first-clear and repeat rewards.
