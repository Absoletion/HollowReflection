# Hollow Reflections — Town and Quest Rewards v0.9

## Castle Town

- **Adventurer's Garrison (Castle):** launches campaign story missions.
- **Wayfarer's Inn:** summons units, opens party management and the Unit Library, and provides party-member conversations.
- **Quest Board:** sits outside the Inn and is reserved for future side and character missions.
- **Market:** displays the player's Gold and reserves a place for future supply and upgrade purchases.
- **Training Yard:** summarizes owned-unit level and XP progress.

## Reward model

Core quest rewards are fixed per quest rather than rolled per enemy. This keeps progression predictable, makes balancing and replay testing straightforward, and prevents farming behavior from depending on enemy spawn composition.

Each battle defines a first-clear and repeat table. Both grant Gold and the listed XP to every participating unit. Repeat values are lower. Guild Sigils are premium-flavored currency and are awarded only on the first clear. A defeat grants nothing; Chapter Three's required scripted loss counts as a story clear.

Enemy-specific drops can be layered onto this model later for Challenge evolution materials without changing the dependable Gold and XP baseline.
