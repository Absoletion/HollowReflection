# Hale, UnHollowed — 5-Star Production Brief

## Identity

- Form: 5-star story evolution of Hale, Adventurer.
- Role: pure Dark/Hollow damage attacker.
- Visible height: 192px on a 256×256 canvas, feet anchored at y=238.
- Battle direction: Southwest is mandatory for production. Other rotations may be inspected diagnostically, but must not replace Southwest.

## Visual progression

- Preserve Hale's silver-white hair, red eyes, neck scar, dark-purple cape, sword-knight silhouette, and asymmetric armor language.
- Armor is heavier and more authoritative than his 4-star form, but must still permit explosive sword movement.
- Dark energy remains black-purple. Controlled Hollow energy is teal-white and appears as fractures through the sword, left gauntlet/arm, and neck scar.
- Hollow influence must read as mastered power, not uncontrolled corruption.
- No shield, tank silhouette, or defensive visual language.

## PixelLab source requirements

- Full-body, exact front view, visually symmetrical stance.
- Weaponless and effect-free.
- Transparent background; no floor, shadow, text, logo, border, aura, or props.
- Anime pixel proportions matching the approved Cinnia and Katie references.
- Fixed 256×256 canvas; visible body height 192px; center x=128; feet y=238.
- At least 8px padding above hair and below feet.

## Kit animation language

- Skill — Fracture Edge: a rapid sword cut that leaves a short teal fracture inside a black-purple edge trail.
- Arts — Event Horizon: Hale compresses Dark energy around the blade, pulls the target line inward, then cleaves through the compressed field with teal cracks at the collapse point.
- Burst — Shatterfall: Hollow Reflection: maximum-impact battlefield attack. Dark space fractures with teal Hollowglass, Hale drives the blade through the fault, and the reflected field collapses across every enemy.
- Every action uses a stable camera, fixed scale, fixed ground line, readable anticipation, explosive release, impact hold, and controlled recovery.
