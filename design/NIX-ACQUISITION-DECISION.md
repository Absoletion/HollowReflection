# Nix acquisition decision (v0.49)

Nix remains a Chapter 3-era unit with no authored recruitment scene in the
approved Chapter 3 outline. For v0.49 she is therefore available from the
standard 3-star pool, with no guaranteed story copy. Existing saves that
already own Nix retain ownership. A future authored medical waystation scene
may add a guaranteed story copy without changing the banner pool.
